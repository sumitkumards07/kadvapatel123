Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Refund Conditions

Our refund policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our management. Refunds are processed based on the timing of the cancellation relative to the scheduled check-in time.

Cancellation Time Refund Amount
At least 48 hours before check-in Full Refund (100%)
Between 48 hours and 24 hours before check-in Partial Refund (50%)
Less than 24 hours before check-in No Refund (0%)

Refund Process

All refund requests must be initiated via the original booking channel (WhatsApp or Website). Once approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within **5-7 business days**.

  • Processing Time: 5-7 Business Days
  • Payment Method: Original payment source (Bank/UPI/Card)
  • Support: For any issues, contact us at +91 92562 03512

Policy Governing

Management reserves the right to modify this policy at any time without prior notice.
