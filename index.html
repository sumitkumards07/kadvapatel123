About Kadva Patel Samaj

Established with a vision to provide a comfortable and welcoming home away from home, Kadva Patel Samaj in Dwarka has been serving community members and pilgrims with dedication for decades.

Our facility is designed to offer a peaceful environment for families, groups, and individuals visiting the holy city. We pride ourselves on maintaining clean, well-equipped accommodations while preserving our rich cultural heritage.
